# Charity Engine — $10,000 by October 4, 2027

The wealth-building work has one terminal value: **$10,000 deployed to animal welfare and people in need by October 4, 2027.**

Set 2026-05-28. 494 days / 70 weeks / ~16.3 months from commit.

## Why this number

Not so small it doesn't matter; not so large it's a fantasy. $606/month average flow to charity is falsifiable along the way — if month 3 = $0 to charity, the engine is broken and the plan changes. The number was decided after an LLM Council surfaced that the charity goal had never been quantified and was secretly the design input for every other decision.

## Revenue requirement (working assumption)

Assuming 25–50% of side-revenue flows to charity:
- 25% to charity → $40K total revenue needed
- 50% to charity → $20K total revenue needed
- 100% (dedicated stream) → $10K revenue needed

At $1,497/client wellness build × 50% margin to charity: **14 clients = $10K**.
At Upwork $50/hr × 50% to charity: **400 billed hours = $10K** (~6 hrs/week).

Both paths clear the bar. The 14-day test (see below) picks the winner.

## The 14-day test (running 2026-05-28 → 2026-06-11)

Two cheap probes in parallel:

**Probe A — Wellness offer test:**
- Free-mock playbook to 50 *warm-adjacent* studios (not 250 cold)
- Rebrand existing Pilates Punx booking flow as demo
- Free 48hr mock as the hook, not a pitch
- Win condition: 1+ closed call at $1,497, or 3+ qualified booked discovery calls

**Probe B — Upwork benchmark:**
- One application sprint (~15 high-quality applications)
- Win condition: 1+ contract signed at ≥$45/hr, or 3+ active interviews

Whichever probe produces closer-to-charity-dollar in 14 days **wins.** Commit fully to it. Drop the other.

If neither hits, re-examine assumptions — the offer or the path is wrong, not the effort.
